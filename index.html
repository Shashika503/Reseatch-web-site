Research Project Scope

Research Problems


Selecting and matching dresses manually from the wardrobe is more time-consuming.

Hard to find the exact dress location in the wardrobe.

Unused clothes are taking up more space in the wardrobe.

Users can not access the dress collection if they are not in the house.

Users have to always manually fit-on dresses to see if it matches perfectly or not.

Research Objectives


To automate the dress selection and matching inside the wardrobe.

To automate dress location detection.

To access the wardrobe dress collection online as a shopping support.

To do virtual dress try-on from inside the wardrobe and give real-time experience in less time.
